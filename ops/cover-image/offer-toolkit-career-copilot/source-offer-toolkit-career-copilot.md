## Core Facts

Offer Toolkit is a Claude skill bundle for job hunting. The repository describes three self-contained sub-skills plus a router:

- `job-description-skill`: decodes job descriptions, compares against a resume, and produces an Offer Strategy Report.
- `resume-skill`: builds or polishes resumes and renders them with 11 print-ready templates.
- `bq-skill`: prepares behavioral interview stories from real experience using structured story-bank workflows.

Shared rules from the repository: never fabricate, ask one question at a time, structure content before rendering.

## Editorial Angle

Write this as a workflow recommendation, not a promise that the tool can secure offers. Emphasize that its value is sequencing: decode JD first, then tailor resume, then prepare interview stories.
